Our Farm Sale Project

Our application will generate revenue from connecting farmers with consumers, whether the consumer is a business or person who is interested in fresh farm produce. A portion of sales that occur on our website will fund our business.

Functional Requirements

Essential
1.	User Login
2.	Administrators
-	Manage all accounts (edit/add/remove accounts)
-	View transaction history
3.	Farmers
-	Manage products (add/edit/remove products)
-	Edit information about their farm (location, hours of operation)
4.	Users (buyers)
-	Browse farms
-	Browse product listings from farms
-	Contact farms for information(email)
-	Edit account information (change password, address)
